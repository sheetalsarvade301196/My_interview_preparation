what is verticle scaling and horizontal scaling
horizontal scaling means adding more servers (or instances) to handle increased load, while vertical scaling means increasing the resources (CPU, RAM, etc.) of an existing server. Essentially, horizontal scaling adds more machines, while vertical scaling makes existing machines more powerful.
